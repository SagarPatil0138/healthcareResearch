<p data-start="518" data-end="540"><strong data-start="518" data-end="540">Market Definition:</strong></p>
<p data-start="542" data-end="1149">Neurofeedback systems are innovative technologies that monitor and regulate brainwave activity to help patients improve cognitive functions, reduce stress, and manage various psychological conditions. These systems are used to treat a variety of disorders, including ADHD, anxiety, depression, PTSD, and sleep disturbances. Neurofeedback uses real-time monitoring of brainwaves to provide feedback to the patient, helping to guide them toward healthier brainwave patterns. The growing recognition of neurofeedback as an effective therapy for mental health and cognitive performance is driving market growth.</p>
