<p data-start="618" data-end="640"><strong data-start="618" data-end="640">Market Definition:</strong></p>
<p data-start="642" data-end="1247">Atrial fibrillation (AF) is a common arrhythmia that affects millions of individuals worldwide. The condition is characterized by an irregular and often rapid heart rate, which can lead to various complications, including stroke, heart failure, and other cardiovascular problems. The <strong data-start="926" data-end="962">Atrial Fibrillation Drugs Market</strong> focuses on the pharmaceutical treatments available to manage and reduce the effects of AF, such as rate-control medications, antiarrhythmic drugs, and anticoagulants. These drugs are critical in reducing the risk of stroke and improving quality of life for patients suffering from AF.</p>
