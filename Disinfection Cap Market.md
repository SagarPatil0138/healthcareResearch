<h3 data-start="906" data-end="931"><strong data-start="910" data-end="931">Market Definition</strong></h3>
<p data-start="933" data-end="1499">Disinfection caps are medical devices designed to sterilize and disinfect the surface of IV ports, syringes, and other medical equipment to reduce the risk of contamination and infections. These caps are usually impregnated with antiseptic agents that disinfect the surface of the port before and after injection, ensuring that harmful pathogens do not enter the bloodstream or contaminate medical instruments. Disinfection caps are crucial in healthcare settings where infection prevention is critical, such as in hospitals, clinics, and other medical environments.</p>
