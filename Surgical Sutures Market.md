<h3 data-start="729" data-end="754"><strong data-start="733" data-end="754">Market Definition</strong></h3>
<p data-start="756" data-end="1367">Surgical sutures are medical devices used to close wounds, incisions, or other body openings during or after surgery. Sutures promote tissue healing by holding the tissue in place and controlling bleeding. Surgical sutures are made of various materials, including natural fibers like silk and synthetic materials like nylon and polyester. Depending on the medical needs, sutures are either absorbable or non-absorbable. The increasing number of surgical procedures, coupled with technological advancements in suture materials and their applications, is contributing to the growth of the surgical sutures market.</p>
