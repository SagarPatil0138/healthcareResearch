<p data-start="725" data-end="1240"><strong data-start="725" data-end="747">Market Definition:</strong> Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support (ACLS) refers to a set of clinical interventions and medical procedures that are used to treat patients suffering from life-threatening cardiac emergencies such as cardiac arrest, stroke, or other cardiovascular events. ACLS includes advanced techniques such as chest compressions, defibrillation, drug administration, and airway management. The demand for ACLS is increasing as the prevalence of heart disease, cardiac arrests, and stroke rises globally.</p>
